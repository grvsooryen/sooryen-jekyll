Mindware is one of the top toy retailers online today. Providing intellectually stimulating toys and games for children of all ages.

Problem

Create and implement a mobile version of their existing desktop platform and implement an alternative payment option for users.

Solution

While working closely with the Mindware team and through the use of Tritium and Moovweb services & technologies, the Sooryen team was able to build and implement a user friendly mobile platform in a two month time frame.

PayPal was added to the site to give another secure payment option that users are familiar with.

Benefits

Sales:
$711,395

Average Order Value:
$64.46

Conversion Rate:
2.17%

Total Sessions:
362,145

There was a total of 362,145 mobile sessions in addition to their desktop platform since and the implementation of Mindwares mobile platform as of October 2014. From those sessions a total mobile revenue of $711,395 have been generated with a conversion rate of 2.17% and an average order value of $64.46.
