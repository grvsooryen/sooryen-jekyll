KYC-FINANCIAL - WHAT’S THAT?

KYC-FINANCIAL uses Distributed Ledgers, a cutting-edge technology, to provide a platform for secure and convenient transactions. Individuals can manage their digital technology securely and businesses can control their client information in an easy and reliable manner.

AUTHETICATED DATA

Distributed Ledger technology provides authenticated documents secured by a collection of cryptographic mechanisms. This ensures that the data cannot be tampered. Consequently, reliable data can be provided for audits and regulatory checks.

COMPLETE COMPLIANCE

KYC-Financial is built with the actual legal framework, as its backbone, regarding identity and customer data at a global scale. Multiple mechanisms are provided to aid institutions to comply with regulatory norms, such as automatic smart checks on external data sources.

DEPENDABLE DATA SHARING

KYC-Financial provides a secure platform for sharing authenticated identity claims, data or documents suitable to satisfy compliance requirements for KYC onboarding or refresh. All this can be achieved seamlessly, without compromising the privacy of the owners and preserving the data integrity.

EASE AND CONVENIENCE

Businesses and Financial Institutions can onboard new customers and refresh data with ease and convenience. KYC-Financial breaks entry barriers by providing a secure mechanism to authenticate digital identity.

KYC-FINANCIAL – WHY US?

Built over Distributed Ledger technology, KYC-Financial provides verified customer data at your finger-tips. It brings a new level trust coupled with ease and simplicity to the onboarding of new customers and refreshing data while ensuring compliance with regulatory standards. Similarly, corporate entities prove the legitimacy of legal entities is also critical. KYC-Financial provides a convenient platform for incorporating companies, by allowing registered agents and owners to share the pertinent documents and get them digitally attested by notaries and institutions.

MASTER OF YOUR IDENTITY

Individual users own the "keys" to their personal data and identity certificates. Therefore, identity owners are the only ones who get to choose which part of their information is to be shared, with whom and under what terms i.e. they can permission the data to be shared with different institutions and business.

SECURED CONTENT - CRYPTOGRAPHY

Strong cryptographic protocols are engaged on the different layers of the platform to achieve the best security, privacy and verifiable proofs of identity secured by the immutability of distributed ledgers.
