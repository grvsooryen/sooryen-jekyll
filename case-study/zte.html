Solution

Our team of designers went to work to create a platform driven entirely by Magento. Once their current system was brought over to the new Magento platform a new theme was in order. Our designers worked closely with ZTE to create a custom theme reflecting the innovative and sleek designs of their technologies. PayPal and other third party payment options were implemented for a more user friendly experience. Designers worked around the clock to ensure the site was optimized on both desktop and mobile platforms in the short time frame allotted.
