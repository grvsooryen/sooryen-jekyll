Ram Ganesan

CEO & Co-Founder

Ram Ganesan is Co-Founder and CEO of Söoryen Technologies with over 20 years of experience in Information Technology, Insurance and E-commerce. Ram founded Söoryen in 2012 to enable CxOs to transform their Financial Services and Ecommerce offerings with cutting edge mobile & cloud technologies. Prior to Söoryen, Ram served as the Vice President of Application Development at 1800Flowers, responsible for oversight of Application Development and Operations for all critical transactional systems. Ram's deep expertise in the area of Insurance products was developed during his 6 years at Metlife Insurance, where he grew into the role of Assistant Vice President of International IT and managed worldwide Application Development and Information Technology for the Insurance Portfolio.
